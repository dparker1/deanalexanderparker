Dean Alexander Parker

I am a PhD candidate in Finance at the NYU Stern School of Business, expected to graduate in 2027. My research sits at the intersection of alternative asset management, climate risk, and financial intermediation.

My job market paper examines conflicts of interest in publicly listed private equity, asking whether private funds can fulfill the transparency obligations of public markets.

Prior to my PhD, I was a Senior Research Analyst at the Federal Reserve Bank of New York, where I contributed to monetary policy research and maintained the FRBNY MBS prepayment model.

I currently live with my beautiful wife, Peiying Yu, in Jersey City, NJ. I am originally from Toms River, NJ (the "Jersey Shore").
